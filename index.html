About Me

I am not a heavy social media user, but my Linkedin is on the contacts page. I enjoy keeping up with Tech news and am super excited that a phenomena called "Room Temperature Superconductivity" was just discovered in 2020. What this discovery has done is open new possibilites to revolutionize technology (yes theres more room for tech to grow). Normal semi-conductors require very cold temperatures to operate thus requiring a lot of energy to keep them cool. Room temperature semi-conductors will not need this extra energy to keep cool.
